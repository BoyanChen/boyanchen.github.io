Introduction

It seems like we know time inherently. Time is linear and one direction, can’t be stopped, speed up or go back. It is absolute concept that we follow everyday in our life. We get up, go to work, study, stay with our friends and lover, and finally we die. Have you ever stopped to try to understand what time truly is? The thing that “pushes” us from born to die. Have you ever asked yourself if there is another possibility to understand time? The answer is yes. The release of the theory of relativity proves Time may be not so absolute as we used to think about it. Inspired by this great theory. I wants to explore the possibility of understanding time different f rom traditioanl way. I want to redesign time.
